Investment Likelihood Calculator

Welcome to the Investment Likelihood Calculator, a comprehensive tool designed to help you assess and enhance your investment prospects. By evaluating various metrics, you can understand where you stand and identify areas for improvement.

This calculator is ideal for use with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems like Odoo and Pipedrive. After assessing an investor, you can easily copy the report and paste it into your CRM for broader visibility among your team members, ensuring everyone is informed and aligned.

To share the report, simply click the "Copy Results to Clipboard" button after completing your assessment, and paste the content into your CRM notes or communication channels.
